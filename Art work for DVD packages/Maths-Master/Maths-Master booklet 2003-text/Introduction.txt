
Introduction

Maths-Master is a revolutionary new study tool developed to empower students and help them master a subject that many people find difficult. 

More than the traditional textbook Maths-Master prompts you through each step of the problem, leading to the correct answer. Guidance is provided by means of reminders, pictures, diagrams and suggestions, leading all the way to the full solutions.

This approach means you have the equivalent of a private tutor on computer disc.

By constant revision and practice you will learn the patterns involved in solving problems and be well prepared for the exam.
